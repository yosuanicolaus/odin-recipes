Bacon-Ranch Chicken Enchiladas

Looks yummy, eh?

Bacon-Ranch Chicken Enchiladas. You can already guess from the food name that this one is gonna be delicious.

Don't worry ladies and gentleman. I will not just show this picture and leave you drooling behind your screen. You can make this dish too. So, without further ado, let's jump into the ingredients!

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • olive oil
  • onion
  • chicken
  • bacon
  • garlic
  • salt, pepper, powder
  • ranch
  • enchilada sauce

Steps

  1. heat oven
  2. heat olive oil, add onion. Stir in chicken, bacon, and garlic. Season with salt, pepper, powder
  3. mix ranch
  4. pour enchilada sauce
  5. bake in oven, about 15 minutes
